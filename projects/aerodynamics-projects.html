Aerodynamics Projects

MATLAB simulations of point‑vortex dynamics and vortex‑panel methods.

Point‑Vortex Dynamics

I developed a MATLAB solver to simulate interacting point vortices using a fourth‑order Runge–Kutta integrator. The solver was validated against test cases including translating vortex pairs, rotating vortex rings and wake roll‑up.

Vortex‑Panel Method

A linearly varying vortex‑panel method was implemented to compute pressure distributions around a circular cylinder for both non‑lifting and lifting cases with 32–128 panels. Numerical C_p curves were compared to analytical solutions, and separation points were predicted using Thwaites’ criterion.

Skills & Insights

These projects improved my understanding of inviscid vortex dynamics, ODE integration, aerodynamic theory and scientific visualisation. They also strengthened my modular MATLAB programming and debugging skills for coupled ODE simulations.
