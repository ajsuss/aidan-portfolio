Finite Element Method Projects

Solving boundary‑value and dynamic problems using FEM.

Scope

Over a series of FEM assignments, I solved boundary‑value problems and dynamic equations using linear, quadratic and cubic elements, adaptive mesh refinement and Newton’s method.

Highlights

I implemented analytical derivations and MATLAB code to solve 1D boundary‑value problems, second‑order ODEs with mixed boundary conditions, potential flow around a NACA 5012 airfoil, wave propagation using the θ‑method and a nonlinear Poisson equation.

Skills

These projects involved deriving weak formulations, assembling global stiffness and mass matrices, enforcing Dirichlet, Neumann and Robin conditions, performing convergence studies and visualising results.

Outcomes

Accurate numerical solutions converged to analytical benchmarks. I produced error analyses, bar charts and animated wave simulations and deepened my understanding of FEM theory and implementation.
